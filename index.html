Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about roofing services in Dallas

How much does roof replacement cost in Dallas?

Roof replacement in Dallas typically costs between $8,000 - $15,000 depending on materials (asphalt shingles, metal, or tile), roof complexity, and home size. We offer free on-site estimates throughout the Dallas area.

How long does a new roof last in Dallas weather?

With proper installation and maintenance, asphalt shingles last 20-30 years, metal roofs 40-70 years, and tile roofs 50+ years in Dallas climate. We use only premium materials rated for Texas weather conditions.

Do you offer emergency roofing services in Dallas?

Yes! We provide 24/7 emergency roof repair across Dallas, Fort Worth, Plano, Frisco, and surrounding areas. Our team responds within 2 hours for urgent leaks and storm damage.

Are your roofers licensed in Texas?

Absolutely. All our installers are TDLR-licensed, fully insured with $2M liability coverage, and trained in Texas building codes. We provide documentation upon request.

How soon can you start my roofing project?

Most projects start within 3-7 days of deposit. Emergency repairs can begin same day. During storm season, we prioritize urgent jobs and schedule accordingly.
